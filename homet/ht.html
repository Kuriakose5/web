About Pallikkara

Pallikkara is a beautiful village located in Ernakulam. Pallikkara is one of the localities in Kochi. The pincode of this locality is 683565. This locality is near Ambalappady, Kizhakkambalam and Kunnathunad. This locality has 30+ properties to buy. The residents of Pallikkara rated this locality at 3.7/5 in terms of connectivity, whereas for safety, they rated it 3.8/5.
