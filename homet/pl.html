About Pallikkara

Pallikkara is a beautiful village located in the southern region of India. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, lush green landscapes, and warm hospitality of the locals. Pallikkara offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity, making it an ideal destination for tourists.

For more information about Pallikkara, you can visit Pallikkara on Wikipedia.

Tourist Attractions

Here are some must-visit tourist attractions in Pallikkara:

  • Pallikkara Bhagavathi Temple - A historic temple known for its architectural beauty.
  • Pallikkara Juma Masjid - A significant religious site for the Muslim community.
  • Pallikkara Panchayat Office - An administrative building with an impressive design.
